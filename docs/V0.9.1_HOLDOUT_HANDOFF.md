## Frozen Gates

Promotion requires every tier and the integrated result to meet all rules:

- mean paired semantic score delta at least 0;
- task-clustered 95% CI lower bound at least -2 points;
- full-pass rate and completion rate no lower than raw;
- paired token ratio at most 1.03;
- paired wall-time ratio at most 1.05;
- timeout and loop incidence no higher than raw;
- no repeated task regression of 10 points or more;
- zero contamination, hidden-test leakage, test edits, missing artifacts, provenance failures,
  unapproved retries, or arm substitution.

A quality-uplift claim additionally requires mean paired score delta at least +2 and a
task-clustered 95% CI lower bound above 0. Passing only the noninferiority gate permits an efficiency
claim, not a quality-uplift claim.

Cutoff slots are scored from the cutoff-time repository state and completion is reported separately.
Infrastructure failures are censored and rerun once in a new sanitized worktree linked to the
original attempt. Model/session limits are infrastructure failures, not zero-quality observations.

## Handoff Boundary

No holdout task existed before the identity above was sealed. Task calibration, sealing, execution,
audit, visualization, and paper updates must occur in the separate benchmark repository. Product
bytes under tag `v0.9.1-rc1` must not change during that work. Any product change creates a new
candidate, invalidates the 240-slot confirmation, and requires a new preregistration.

Billable slot execution requires separate explicit approval. Until all 240 valid slots and the
fail-closed audit complete, v0.8.0 remains stable and v0.9.1 quality uplift remains unproven.
