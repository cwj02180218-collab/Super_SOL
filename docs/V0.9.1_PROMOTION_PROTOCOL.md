# Super SOL v0.9.1 Promotion Protocol

Status: preregistered before product-candidate benchmarking.

## Scope

Gate 0 freezes the product candidate without billable model execution. Gate 1 compares stock Codex
with Super SOL v0.9.1-rc1 on 30 new tasks that were not used to design v0.9.1. The old T201-T230
pack is diagnostic-only and cannot support promotion.

The crossover matrix is:

- raw and candidate under Sol/high;
- raw and candidate under Terra/xhigh;
- two independent repetitions for every task and arm;
- 30 tasks x 2 model tiers x 2 arms x 2 repetitions = 240 valid slots.

Each slot starts in fresh homes and fresh worktrees. Model, effort, prompt, repository seed,
environment, timeout, grader, and tool availability are identical inside each paired comparison.
Only the plugin is changed. Hidden semantic graders remain outside model context.

## Gate 0

The candidate must pass the complete test suite with at least 90% coverage, Ruff, format checking,
BasedPyright, package build and archive inventory, dependency and secret audit, loop replay, hook
latency limits, and stock Codex hook lifecycle checks. No candidate code may change after Gate 0;
any product change creates a new candidate and invalidates later observations.

## Gate 1

Promotion requires all of the following within each model tier and in the integrated result:

- mean paired score delta at least 0;
- task-clustered 95% CI lower bound at least -2;
- full-pass rate and completion rate no lower than raw;
- paired token ratio at most 1.03;
- paired wall-time ratio at most 1.05;
- timeout and loop incidence no higher than raw;
- no repeated task regression of 10 points or more;
- zero contamination, hidden-test leakage, test edits, missing artifacts, provenance failures,
  unapproved retries, or arm substitution.

Quality uses semantic grader scores only. Process compliance is reported separately and cannot lift
the quality score. A cutoff slot is graded from its cutoff-time repository state and completion is
reported separately; it is not assigned an automatic zero. Infrastructure failures are censored and
replayed in a new sanitized worktree with an explicit link to the original record.

A quality uplift requires a mean paired score delta of at least +2 and a task-clustered 95% CI lower
bound above 0. Passing noninferiority without this stronger rule permits an efficiency claim only,
not a quality-uplift claim.

## Execution Authority

Gate 1 requires separate explicit billable approval after Gate 0 artifacts and hashes are frozen.
The harness performs no automatic model calls, retries, continuations, model changes, or subagent
starts. Stable `v0.9.1` cannot be tagged unless all promotion rules pass.
